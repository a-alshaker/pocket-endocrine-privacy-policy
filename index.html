Privacy Policy for Pocket Endocrine

Last updated: October 29, 2025

Pocket Endocrine (“the App”) is provided as an educational tool to support the understanding and review of endocrine and diabetes-related conditions, along with their management. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle any information related to your use of the App.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The App does not request or require any information such as your name, email address, location, or usage data.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools that might collect or process user data.

3. User Accounts

The App does not require users to create an account or sign in. All features are accessible without registration or login.

4. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

5. Data Security

As no personal data is collected, there are no specific data security measures in place. The App is designed for offline use, and no data is transmitted to any servers.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document, and the date of the latest update will be shown at the top.
